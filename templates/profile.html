Company Profile

The Dawn

In the days when the idea of manufacturing Poly Viscose Suiting was a unique concept in India, BSL Limited was born in the desert state of Rajasthan in the year 1971 and the town where this happened was Bhilwara.
Over the years with the belief that there are no shortcuts to excellence, BSL has moved from strength to strength today as a multi-million dollar company on the international map of premium suitings. We are one of the leading manufacturers & exporters of Poly Viscose and Poly Wool Suitings fabric.
Similarly in 2005, when the concept of Silk fabrics and Furnishing fabrics was still new to the town Bhilwara, the company diversified into furnishing and suitings fabrics with the manufacturing of huge Silk fabrics and today it is one of the leading manufacturers and exporters of furnishing fabrics from India with a diverse range in various synthetic, blended and silk fabrics.
With an experience of almost 5 decades in textile manufacturing, BSL is currently producing over 20.00 million meters of fabric every year. The product range covers Pure Wool / Poly Viscose/ Poly Wool suitings fabric along with synthetic blended and silk fabrics for furnishing. BSL today has a presence in 60 countries over the world and in every state and district in India.The company has earned numerous awards for its outstanding export performance.
BSL products are manufactured under ISO / ISO 9001. It also has various international certifications such as OEKOTEX, VEGAN etc.

The Vision

The first step towards any big achievement is the vision behind it. Every big success story was first a dream in someone's mind. Every tree was first a small seed.
BSL has survived the test of times and and in today's challenging dynamic scenario of international business has emerged as a winner which is largely due to its vision towards growth and commitment towards quality & excellence. BSL continues to expand its horizons and with its visionary leadership and constantly strives to further consolidate its position in the textile market and emerge as a leader in both its segments if suiting's & furnishing domestically and globally.
